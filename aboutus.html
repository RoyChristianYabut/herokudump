About Us

ROY Plaza Corporation humbly started in 1978 with a registry name "ROY Bargain Center", a single proprietorship business engaged in retailing dry goods at Dipolog City. Ten years later, they were able to own new property. On that property, they constructed a 4-story building along Rizal Avenue, Dipolog City, and gave the business a new name, "Roy Plaza".

ROY Plaza corporation was approved by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in the year 1996. In the year 1998, the corporation was able to acquire property in Valencia, Bukidnon. Moreover, the business continued to flourish, and started to engage in selling grocery items, and created a loyalty program for their customers. Despite the challenges, ROY Plaza continued to grow by God’s grace, together with hard work, patience, faith, and obedience to God. Out of the overflow of God’s blessings, the company started giving school supplies to different schools in Valencia City eight years ago.

ROY Plaza faced another challenge in March 2020 when the Philippines experienced a tremendous outbreak that affected the lives of Filipinos-- including businesses and other establishments. To prevent the spread of COVID-19, the president declared a general community quarantine in the country, which also affected people’s shopping behavior.

The Valencia City participated in the ECQ and declared a lockdown wherein no outsiders are allowed to pass through Valencia City, senior citizen and children with pre-existing diseases are not allowed to go out, and the implementation of curfew time at 8:00 pm-5:00 am. As more cities are under lockdowns, nonessential establishments are ordered to close, and customers were avoiding public places. During this season, people are requesting for online shopping ready for delivery and pick-up. With this, ROY Plaza Store comes up with a solution to provide an online one-stop marketplace, in a modernized, tech-savvy, and friendly way, which is very timely for the customer's needs.

ROY Plaza Store aims to provide the customers with a firm range of services and products that makes the customer’s shopping experience easier. Hence, ROY Plaza Store envision a world where all people have what they need.
