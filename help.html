How to be a member of ROY Plaza Store Online?

Registering for ROY Plaza Store Online is easy! Be a member of ROY Plaza Smart Shoppers Club and register using your e-mail address! To get started, simply follow the steps below

Step 1

Be a member of the ROY Plaza Smart Shoppers Club. To register sign up at the ROY Plaza CWD area by filling up the important details provided in the form.

Step 2

Go to www.royplazastore.com and click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner of the page.

Step 3

Fill in the required details. Make sure that your contact details (emails or phone number) are active so that order updates and other communications will be sent to you.

Step 4

Complete the registration by creating your ROY Plaza Store username and password


How to activate account

1 Go to www.royplazastore.com
2 Log in to your username and password
3 Fill in the required details
4 Enter your SSC Card number to completely activate your account
